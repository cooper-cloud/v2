LIFE Exemption
Listed Issuer Financing Exemption — a Canadian regulation allowing listed companies to raise capital faster via a public offering document without a full prospectus.
Price / Unit
The price at which you subscribe to receive shares (and warrants if included). Set by the company at time of offering.
Warrant Strike
The price at which you can buy additional shares using your warrant. Typically set 30–50% above the offering price.
Warrant Expiry
Warrants expire worthless if not exercised by this date. Junior mining warrants typically run 18–24 months.
Lead Broker
The investment dealer running the placement. Contact them directly to subscribe — or use the Secure Access link to request an introduction through Copper Brief.
Request Intro
Use this to signal interest via Copper Brief. We'll forward your details to the IR team or broker where appropriate.
