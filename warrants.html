Leverage
Stock price ÷ warrant price. A 4x leverage means if the stock doubles, the warrant can 4x or more.
Strike Price
The price you pay per share when you exercise the warrant. Below this = out of the money.
In the Money (ITM)
Stock price is above the strike. The warrant has intrinsic value you can realise today.
Liquidity Warning
TSXV warrants trade thin — often under 25K units/day. Use limit orders. Check bid/ask in IBKR before sizing.
