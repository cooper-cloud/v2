About Copper Brief

BUILT FOR
DEAL FLOW.

Copper Brief is built by Cooper John — a junior mining capital markets researcher and media operator focused on the TSXV. Copper Brief is Canada's only dedicated LIFE financing portal, combining real-time deal tracking with CEO interviews and company research.

The LIFE tracker exists because institutional-grade deal data should be accessible to all investors, not just those with Bloomberg terminals. The video content exists because the most useful conversations happen when the interviewer understands warrant terms, dilution mechanics, and what a LIFE offering document actually says.

Every data point is sourced directly from SEDAR+ offering documents. Every interview is conducted with a focus on capital structure and catalysts — not talking points.

WHAT COPPER BRIEF TRACKS

01
LIFE Financing Tracker

Every Listed Issuer Financing Exemption filed on SEDAR+ by TSXV-listed mining companies. Offering date, price per unit, warrant terms, lead broker, and direct link to the filing.

02
Warrant Terms

Strike price, expiry date, and whether warrants are included in the offering. Tracked across all active LIFE deals so you can assess the opportunity before the offering closes.

03
CEO Interviews

Long-form conversations with junior mining executives. Capital structure, project fundamentals, upcoming catalysts. Published on YouTube and archived here.

04
Deal Flow Access

Use the Secure Access feature on the tracker to register interest in active LIFE offerings. Copper Brief facilitates direct introductions to the lead broker or IR team managing each deal.
